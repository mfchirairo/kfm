About us

KFM is an e-learning platform meant to help people of all ages get access to the much needed education. Teachers, students and anybody willing to at least learn or contribute something educational, feel free to visit KFM. Your hobby is reading, we have you covered as well. Getting the right material to help in our studies at times comes at a cost, so most educational materials are free except for a few materials we also acquire at a cost. We also have actual books on offer for short term or long term hire. You have a book not currently in use, contact us so we can get it hired. We strive to make education accessible to all. We won`t stop till we reach that goal. You want to teach anything online or you have special online or e-learning requirements feel free to contact us.
